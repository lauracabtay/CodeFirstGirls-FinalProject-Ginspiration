Fruity

A fruity gin and tonic is the ideal party drink to enjoy in the summer. For a refreshingly vibrant G&T, we recommend to pick an uncomplicated gin with more neutral notes such as a London Dry-style gin. It is ideal for infusing fruits as it takes on flavours better. Sloe

A Sloe Gin and Tonic is very similar to the traditional G&T, but it includes sloe gin, which is a gin flavored with sloe berries. The result is a cocktail that's more tart and colorful than the original, while still providing the refreshment you expect from your Gin and Tonics.
